FastHenry2 User Guide

Welcome to FastHenry2 !

FastHenry2 is a powerful three-dimensional inductance extraction program.

Features include:

  • Computation of frequency dependent self and mutual inductances between conductors of complex shape!
  • Automatic generation of Spice netlist to model and simulate the frequency depended behaviour of your structure!
  • Very fast solution!
  • Full automation support allowing remote control of FastHenry2 by other programs, either in foreground or in background!
  • Lots of options!
  • Free (see disclaimer and conditions of use)!
